Pasta with Sausage and Ricotta

Ingredients

- [ ] 150g pasta
- [ ] 250g fresh salsiccia
- [ ] 250g ricotta
- [ ] 2 shallots, finely chopped
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] Zest of half a lemon
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] ½ tsp black pepper
- [ ] 30g Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
- [ ] Fresh basil or parsley (optional)

Instructions

- [ ] Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook the pasta until al dente.
      Reserve about 250ml of pasta water before draining.
- [ ] While pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high
      heat. Add shallots and cook until softened (2-3 minutes). Remove sausage
      from casing, add to pan, break into small pieces, and cook until browned
      (6-8 minutes).
- [ ] Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
- [ ] In a bowl, mix ricotta with lemon zest, salt, and pepper. Add 3-4 tbsp of
      pasta water and stir until creamy.
- [ ] Add drained pasta to the pan with sausage and toss. Remove from heat, add
      ricotta mixture, and toss well. Add more pasta water (60-125ml) as needed
      for a creamy sauce.
- [ ] Serve with Parmesan and herbs if desired.
