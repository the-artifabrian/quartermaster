Ciorba de varza

Ingredients

- [x] 200 g pancetta, diced
- [x] 2 tbsp oil or lard (only if pancetta is lean)
- [x] 1 large onion, finely chopped
- [x] 2 cloves garlic, minced
- [x] 1 tsp caraway seeds
- [x] 1 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
- [x] 2 tbsp tomato paste
- [x] 1 medium carrot, diced
- [x] 1 small green/white cabbage (600–800 g), cored, thinly sliced
- [x] 1.5 L chicken or pork stock (or water + stock cube)
- [x] 2 bay leaves
- [ ] 150 ml sour cream (tejföl-style), room temp
- [ ] 1 tbsp flour (for tempering; optional)
- [ ] 1–2 tsp white wine vinegar or lemon juice (to taste)
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] Black pepper, to taste
- [ ] Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

- [ ] Render pancetta in a pot over medium heat until lightly crisp; remove some
      fat if excessive.
- [ ] Add onion; sauté until translucent, 5–7 min.
- [ ] Stir in garlic and caraway; cook 30 sec.
- [ ] Pull pot off heat; stir in paprika to bloom (avoid burning).
- [ ] Add tomato paste, cook for 1 min.
- [ ] Return to heat; add carrot and potato; toss 1–2 min.
- [ ] Add cabbage; toss to coat in fat and spices, 2–3 min.
- [ ] Pour in stock; add bay leaf; bring to a boil.
- [ ] Reduce to a simmer; cover and cook until cabbage is tender, 20–25 min.
- [ ] Season with salt and pepper; adjust liquid if needed.
