Stir-Fried Vermicelli with Pancetta, Veg, and Soy–Oyster Sauce (serves 2)

Ingredients

- [ ] 180 g dry rice vermicelli
- [ ] 120 g pancetta, cubed
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 2 cups veg total: broccoli (small florets + julienned stem), shredded
      cabbage, 1 carrot matchsticked
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp ginger, minced
- [ ] 1–2 tbsp neutral oil (as needed)  
       Sauce (mix well)
- [ ] 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
- [ ] 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- [ ] 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- [ ] 1 tsp rice vinegar
- [ ] 1 tsp sugar
- [ ] 3 tbsp water
- [ ] 1/2–1 tsp sriracha
- [ ] 1/4 tsp white or black pepper
- [ ] 1 tsp cornstarch

Instructions

- [ ] Noodles: Soak vermicelli in very hot water 5–8 min until pliable; drain
      well and toss with 1 tsp oil.
- [ ] Pancetta: Medium heat, cook 3–5 min until lightly crisp; leave ~1–1.5 tbsp
      fat in pan, reserve pancetta.
- [ ] Aromatics/veg: Add garlic and ginger 20 sec. Add carrot and broccoli stems
      1 min; add florets and cabbage 2–3 min (splash 1–2 tbsp water if needed).
      Veg should be crisp-tender.
- [ ] Eggs: Push veg aside, add a few drops oil; scramble eggs just set, then
      mix through.
- [ ] Noodles + sauce: Add noodles and pancetta. Pour in sauce around edges.
      Toss 1–2 min to coat and heat. If you want it glossy, add slurry and toss
      30–60 sec.
- [ ] Balance: Taste—add a splash soy for salt, vinegar for brightness, or
      sriracha for heat. Finish with pepper.
