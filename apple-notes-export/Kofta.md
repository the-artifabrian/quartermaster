Kofta

Ingredients

- [ ] 700g ground beef shoulder
- [ ] 1tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 1 small onion, grated
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, grated (or 1tsp garlic powder)
- [ ] 1 bunch chopped fresh parsley
- [ ] 3/4 tsp cumin
- [ ] 1 tsp coriander
- [ ] 2 tsp paprika
- [ ] 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] black pepper

Instructions

- [ ] Grate the onion into the bowl using a standard box grater. Add remaining
      Kofta ingredients and mix well with your hands. 
- [ ] With damp hands, divide the meat into 8 equal pieces and push and shape
      the meat onto 8 skewers. Make them smooth (looks nicer, pictured!) or a
      bit nubbly (more surface area to brown!). 
- [ ] Preheat 1/2 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Cook in
      batches for 5 minutes in total, turning to brown evenly all over.
