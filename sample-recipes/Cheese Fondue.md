# Cheese Fondue

Ingredients

- [ ] 200g gruyere
- [ ] 200g emmental
- [ ] 160ml dry white wine
- [ ] 1 garlic clove
- [ ] 1 tbsp corn starch
- [ ] 1 tsp lemon juice
- [ ] freshly ground black pepper
- [ ] grated nutmeg

Instructions

- [ ] Grate the gruyere and emmental; toss with the cornstarch until evenly
      coated.
- [ ] Cut the garlic clove in half and rub the inside of your pot (or fondue
      pot) with it.
- [ ] Pour in the white wine and lemon juice; warm gently over medium-low until
      steaming (don’t boil).
- [ ] Add the cheese a handful at a time, stirring constantly in a figure‑8
      until each addition melts before adding more.
- [ ] Keep heat low and stir until smooth and glossy; if it tightens, keep
      stirring gently (a tiny splash more wine only if needed).
- [ ] Season with black pepper and a pinch of grated nutmeg; serve immediately
      and keep it just barely simmering.
