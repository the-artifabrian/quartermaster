Eggs Benedict with Béarnaise Sauce

Ingredients

- [ ] 4 slices sourdough bread or 2 English muffins
- [ ] 4 large eggs
- [ ] 4 slices prosciutto cotto or 8 slices bacon
- [ ] 1 tbsp vinegar (optional, for poaching)
- [ ] 114g butter (adjust as needed)
- [ ] 4 egg yolks
- [ ] 2 tbsp white wine vinegar or lemon juice
- [ ] 2 tbsp dry white wine
- [ ] 1 small shallot, finely minced
- [ ] 2 tbsp fresh tarragon, chopped (divided)
- [ ] 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped (optional)
- [ ] Salt and white pepper to taste

Instructions  
For the Béarnaise sauce:

- [ ] In a small saucepan, combine white wine vinegar, white wine, minced
      shallot, and 1 tbsp of the chopped tarragon. Bring to a simmer over medium
      heat and reduce until about 1-2 tablespoons of liquid remain. Strain the
      reduction and let it cool slightly.
- [ ] Melt the butter in a separate small saucepan until hot but not browned.
- [ ] In a small bowl, beat the egg yolks. Add the strained reduction to the egg
      yolks and whisk to combine.
- [ ] Slowly add the hot melted butter to the egg mixture in a thin stream,
      whisking constantly to emulsify. Continue until all butter is incorporated
      and the sauce is thick and smooth.
- [ ] Remove from heat and stir in the remaining chopped tarragon and chervil
      (if using). If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of cream to thin it.
      Season with salt and white pepper to taste.

To poach the eggs:

- [ ] Fill a medium-sized pot with about 3 inches of water. Bring the water to a
      boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer (small bubbles should rise to the
      surface, but the water should not roll).
- [ ] Add a splash of vinegar to the water (optional, to help the egg whites
      stay together).
- [ ] Crack one egg into a small cup or ramekin. Gently lower the egg into the
      simmering water, easing it out of the cup.
- [ ] Cook the egg for 3–5 minutes, depending on how soft you want the yolk.
      Remove the poached egg with a slotted spoon.
- [ ] Repeat with the remaining eggs.

To prepare the bacon/prosciutto:

- [ ] While the eggs are poaching, heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook
      the slices of prosciutto cotto for about 1 minute on each side, or until
      lightly browned. If using bacon, cook for 3-5 minutes until crisp.

To assemble:

- [ ] Toast the sourdough bread or English muffins. Top each toasted slice with
      a piece of prosciutto cotto or bacon, followed by a poached egg.
- [ ] Spoon the Béarnaise sauce generously over the top. Garnish with additional
      fresh herbs, if desired.
- [ ] Serve immediately and enjoy!
