Strawberry Jelly Cake

Ingredients  
Strawberry jelly

- [ ] 700-800g fresh or frozen strawberries/blackberries/raspberries
- [ ] 50g granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon lemon juice (or orange juice)
- [ ] lemon/orange zest
- [ ] optional cinnamon/ginger/clove

Cake

- [ ] 240g all purpose flour
- [ ] 1 ½ teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon baking soda
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
- [ ] 200g granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon lemon zest from about 2 lemons
- [ ] 2 large eggs room temperature
- [ ] 120ml full fat plain yogurt
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ++[pure vanilla extract](https://www.amazon.com/Simply-Organic-Vanilla-Extract-Packaging/dp/B0BK1TRFXB?crid=1NCWYBBK5YLPX&keywords=bourbon+vanilla+extract&qid=1679083122&sprefix=bourbon+vanilla+extract%2Caps%2C107&sr=8-12&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=7054b12999b042a1653150e28fe99df8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++
- [ ] 60ml sunflower oil
- [ ] 60g unsalted butter, melted
- [ ] 80ml milk, room temperature
- [ ] 80ml strawberry compote

Frosting

- [ ] 200ml cold heavy whipping cream
- [ ] 20g granulated sugar
- [ ] 1 tbsp strawberry compote
- [ ] 1 tsp bloomed gelatin

Instructions

- [ ] Make the strawberry compote by simmering strawberries, sugar, and lemon
      until thick, then cool and reserve some for the cake and 1 tbsp for the
      frosting.
- [ ] Mix the cake batter (wet + compote, then fold in dry), bake at 175°C until
      done, and cool completely.
- [ ] Pour cooled-but-still-liquid strawberry jelly over the cooled cake and
      chill until fully set.
- [ ] Make the topping by whipping cream + sugar to soft peaks, streaming in
      melted lukewarm bloomed gelatin, then mixing in 1 tbsp thick compote to
      medium peaks.
- [ ] Pipe the stabilized cream onto the set jelly (optionally brushed with a
      thin tacky layer of compote) and chill 30–60 minutes to lock it in place.
