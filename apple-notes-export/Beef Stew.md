Beef Stew

Ingredients

- [ ] 900 g beef stew meat, 1-inch cubes
- [ ] 1.5 tsp kosher salt, plus more to taste
- [ ] 0.5 tsp freshly ground black pepper
- [ ] 2 tbsp neutral oil
- [ ] 1 large yellow onion, cut into chunks
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato paste
- [ ] 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- [ ] 240 ml dry red wine
- [ ] 1 can (440–500 ml) Guinness (or other stout)
- [ ] 0.5 tsp dried thyme (or 1 tsp fresh)
- [ ] 2 bay leaves
- [ ] 450 g baby white potatoes, halved/quartered
- [ ] 4 medium carrots, peeled and sliced on a diagonal
- [ ] 3 celery ribs, chopped
- [ ] Optional: chopped parsley or fresh thyme for garnish
- [ ] Optional: 1tsp soy sauce

Instructions

- [ ] Pat beef dry; season with 1.5 tsp salt + 0.5 tsp pepper.
- [ ] Heat 2 tbsp oil (Dutch oven, med-high). Sear beef in batches, 2–3
      min/side; set aside.
- [ ] Add onion (pinch salt); cook 3–4 min. Add garlic; 30–60 sec. Stir in 1
      tbsp tomato paste; 1 min.
- [ ] Sprinkle 2 tbsp flour; stir 30–60 sec.
- [ ] Pour in 240 ml red wine; scrape fond; simmer 4–5 min until reduced.
- [ ] Add 1 can Guinness.
- [ ] Add 0.5 tsp dried thyme + 2 bay leaves. Return beef and juices; stir.
- [ ] Add hot water as needed to just covere the beef.
- [ ] Bring to gentle boil; reduce to low. Partially cover; simmer 75-90 min,
      stir occasionally until beef is nearly tender.
- [ ] Optional: add 1tsp soy sauce at this point.
- [ ] Add potatoes, carrots, celery; simmer 20–30 min until tender.
- [ ] Adjust thickness by reducing uncovered if needed. Season to taste.
- [ ] Optional: stir in 1–2 tsp vinegar off heat to brighten.
- [ ] Remove bay leaves. Garnish (parsley/thyme). Serve.
