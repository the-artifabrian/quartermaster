Apple Toddy

Ingredients

- [ ] 240g ounces apple cider
- [ ] 2 teaspoon lemon juice
- [ ] 2 teaspoons honey
- [ ] 1 cinnamon stick
- [ ] 60ml ounces bourbon
- [ ] (optional) orange peel, 1-2 whole cloves

Instructions

- [ ] Combine the apple cider, lemon juice, honey, and cinnamon stick in a small
      saucepan.
- [ ] Bring to a boil over medium-low heat, then reduce heat and simmer for
      about five minutes.
- [ ] Remove from heat, stir in the bourbon, and serve immediately.
