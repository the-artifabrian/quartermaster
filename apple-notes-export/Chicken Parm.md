Chicken Parm

Ingredients

FOR THE CHICKEN CUTLET

- [ ] 600-800g boneless skinless chicken breasts
- [ ] [100g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/92) panko breadcrumbs
- [ ] [10g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/115) or
      [1/2 tsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/122) each (salt,
      pepper, red pepper flake, garlic powder)
- [ ] [1 tsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/183) dried italian
      seasoning

FOR THE SAUCE

- [ ] ¼ red onion, grated
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- [ ] [30g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/276) butter
- [ ] [5g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/288) salt
- [ ] [40g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/296) tomato paste
- [ ] [2 400g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/313) can crushed tomatos
- [ ] [½ tsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/340) dried oregano
- [ ] [½ tsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/360) dried basil
- [ ] [5g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/379) sugar

TO BREAD + FRY CUTLETS

- [ ] 3 eggs whisked
- [ ] [120g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/427) flour
- [ ] [3 tbsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/438) olive oil

CHEESE TOPPING

- [ ] [100g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/472) grated full fat aged
      mozzarella cheese
- [ ] [100g](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/516) grated parmesan
      (smallest holes on box grater)
- [ ] Toss to combine

Instructions

Sauce

- [ ] Heat a sautee pan over medium heat
- [ ] Add butter, onion, garlic, salt
- [ ] Let sautee for 3-4 mins until they start to become translucent and take on
      color
- [ ] Add in tomato paste and cook until it takes on some color
- [ ] Add crushed tomatoes and stir to combine
- [ ] Add oregano, basil, sugar. Stir to combine and reduce heat to low
- [ ] Simmer over low for about 20 minutes until thickened

Cutlets

- [ ] Cut breasts in half horizontally, then pound into about 1/2" thick cutlets
      with mallet or saucepan
- [ ] Season both sides with roughly
      [1 tsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/1111) salt and pepper
- [ ] Cure in fridge 30 minutes
- [ ] Combine breading ingredients, and then bread cutlets starting with flour,
      then egg, then breading.
- [ ] Heat [3 tbsp](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/1267) olive oil in
      large flat bottomed pan
- [ ] Pan fry on medium high for 2-3 minutes per side until golden and crisp.
- [ ] Top each cutlet with sauce and cheese mixture
- [ ] Broil on hi for 2-3 minutes until cutlets is bubbly and fully melted.
