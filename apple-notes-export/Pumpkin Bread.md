Pumpkin Bread

Ingredients

- [ ] 80g unsalted butter
- [ ] 350g pure pumpkin puree
- [ ] 125g packed light or dark brown sugar
- [ ] 2 large eggs at room temperature
- [ ] 80g full fat plain yogurt
- [ ] 80g whole milk
- [ ] 1 teaspoon ++[pure vanilla extract](https://www.amazon.com/Simply-Organic-Vanilla-Extract-Packaging/dp/B0BK1TRFXB?crid=1NCWYBBK5YLPX&keywords=bourbon+vanilla+extract&qid=1679083122&sprefix=bourbon+vanilla+extract%2Caps%2C107&sr=8-12&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=7054b12999b042a1653150e28fe99df8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++
- [ ] 235g all-purpose flour
- [ ] 135g granulated sugar
- [ ] ¾ teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] ¾ teaspoon baking soda
- [ ] 2 teaspoon ++[pumpkin pie spice](https://amzn.to/4f2zv6W)++
- [ ] ½ teaspoon salt
- [ ] 50g ++[dark chocolate](https://www.amazon.com/Guittard-Extra-Dark-Chocolate-11-5/dp/B00IRRU5T4?crid=1TTG6NEHC5EVM&keywords=lindt+bittersweet+chocolate+chips&qid=1662485634&sprefix=lindt+bittersweet+chocolate+chips%2Caps%2C88&sr=8-4&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=6811d7eeb7a33f7118500f6c75a74383&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++,
      chopped
- [ ] 100g roasted pecans, pea-sized

Instructions

- [ ] Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F. Line
      a ++[9x5-inch loaf pan](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B07QR1SQ37/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B07QR1SQ37&linkCode=as2&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=04f7c048d3c2afde8baddd636cbcbf09%22%3EFarberware%20Nonstick%20Bakeware%20Meatloaf/Nonstick%20Baking%20Loaf%20Pan%20-%209%20Inch%20x%205%20Inch,%20Rose%20Gold)++ with
      parchment paper. 
- [ ] First brown the butter. Follow the instructions
      in ++[this post here to learn how to brown butter](https://scientificallysweet.com/how-to-brown-butter/)++.
      It’s easy! Brown specks and a dense golden foam will form at the surface
      when it is nearly ready. Pour it out into a clean bowl and let it cool for
      10 minutes. 
- [ ] Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, pumpkin spice and salt into a
      large bowl and whisk to blend evenly. Add granulated sugar and whisk to
      blend well. 
- [ ] Combine pumpkin puree, brown sugar, eggs, yogurt, milk, vanilla and
      slightly warm (liquid) browned butter in another large bowl and whisk
      until smooth.
- [ ] Make a well in the dry ingredients and add the wet ingredients. Fold it
      all together. Before all of the flour is incorporated, add the chocolate
      chips and pecans and continue to fold gently until evenly combined.
- [ ] Pour the batter into the prepared pan, spread it out evenly and then and
      run a line down the center using edge of the spatula or a butter knife
      (this helps to create that nice crack down the middle of the loaf). Bake
      for 50-60 minutes until it feels firm in the middle and skewer inserted
      into the center comes out without any wet batter. 
- [ ] Transfer the loaf to a wire rack to cool for 20 minutes, then lift it out
      of the pan using the parchment overhand and let it continue cooling on the
      rack.
