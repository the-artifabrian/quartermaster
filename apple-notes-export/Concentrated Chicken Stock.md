Concentrated Chicken Stock

Ingredients

- [ ] 1.5–2.0 kg chicken backs/necks/wings
- [ ] 1 onion, halved
- [ ] 1 carrot, cut in big chunks (optional)
- [ ] 1 celery stalk, chunked (optional)
- [ ] 1–2 bay leaves (optional)
- [ ] Water (cold), to cover and stay below Max line
- [ ] No salt!

Instructions

- [ ] Load pot: Put chicken parts in the liner. Add onion (and carrot/celery if
      using). Add bay/peppercorns if using.
- [ ] Add water: Pour in cold water just to cover the solids, keeping below the
      Max line.
- [ ] Pressure cook: Lid on, valve Sealing. Cook High Pressure 45 minutes (or 60
      minutes for maximum extraction).
- [ ] Release:Natural release 20–30 minutes, then vent remaining pressure.
- [ ] Strain: Strain through a fine mesh strainer into a bowl/pot. Discard
      solids. (Optional: strain again for clearer stock.)
- [ ] Chill + defat: Cool quickly, refrigerate overnight, then lift off the fat
      cap (save if desired).
- [ ] Portion + freeze: Fill 350 ml containers leaving 1–1.5 cm headspace. Label
      and freeze.
