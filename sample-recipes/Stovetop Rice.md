# Stovetop Rice  
  
## Ingredients  
- [ ] 150g jasmine rice  
- [ ] 185g water  
- [ ] 1/4 tsp salt  
  
  
  
## Method   
- [ ] Rinse rice 3–6 times until water is mostly clear. Drain very well.  
- [ ] Add rice + 180 g water (+ salt) to a pot.  
- [ ] Soak for 15 minutes.  
- [ ] Heat on high until a full boil.  
- [ ] Stir once, cover tightly, reduce to very low (gentle simmer) and cook 12 min.  
- [ ] Turn heat off. Rest 10 min, covered.  
- [ ] Fluff with a fork/rice paddle.
