Poolish Naan

Ingredients

Poolish

- [ ] Water - 250g
- [ ] Yeast - 0.4g
- [ ] Honey - 1tsp
- [ ] Flour - 250g

Final Dough

- [ ] Milk - 180g
- [ ] Salt - 12g
- [ ] Yeast - 0.9g
- [ ] Flour - 400g
- [ ] Oil/ghee - 25g

Garlic butter

- [ ] 60g butter
- [ ] 3-4 cloves of garlic
- [ ] a bunch of parsley

Instructions

Poolish (night before)

- [ ] Whisk 250g water + 0.4g yeast + 1 tsp honey, then mix in 250g flour to a
      smooth paste; cover and ferment 8–12h at 20–24°C.

Final dough

- [ ] In a large bowl, add 200g milk.
- [ ] Add poolish + 25g oil/ghee + 0.9g yeast + 12g salt; stir with a fork until
      evenly combined.
- [ ] Add 400 g flour; mix to a shaggy dough, then use a wet hand to mix until
      mostly uniform; cover and rest 10–15 min.
- [ ] Stretch & fold 1.
- [ ] Stretch & fold 2.
- [ ] Shape into a tight ball, put in a lightly oiled container, cover, and bulk
      ferment 2h at room temp.
- [ ] Divide into 120-125g portions, ball each, and place in a dough tray or
      lightly oiled containers.
- [ ] Rest for 1 hour, then stretch and bake in a very hot cast iron skillet or
      pizza oven.
- [ ] Brush with garlic butter mixture while still warm (not hot).
