Chicken kofta

Ingredients

Kofta

- [ ] 900g ground chicken
- [ ] 1 tbsp tomato paste
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 2 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 2 tsp cumin powder
- [ ] 1 tsp ground coriander
- [ ] 1.5 tsp table salt
- [ ] 1/2 tsp black pepper
- [ ] 1 tsp turmeric
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped
- [ ] 1 large or 2 medium yellow onions, grated + squeezed dry

Fresh Salad

- [ ] 1/2 large white or red onion, thinly sliced
- [ ] 1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
- [ ] 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- [ ] 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper & 2 tsp sumac
- [ ] Juice of 1/2 lemon

Instructions

Make the kofta mixture

- [ ] Grate the onion and squeeze it very dry.
- [ ] In a bowl, mix: 900 g ground chicken, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 4 minced garlic
      cloves, 2 tsp smoked paprika, 2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, 1.5 tsp table
      salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1/3 cup
      chopped parsley, squeezed onion, and 1 tbsp olive oil. Mix 2–3 minutes
      until tacky.  
       Shape and bake
- [ ] Lightly oil or line a sheet pan. Spread mixture into a 1.25 cm (1/2-inch)
      thick rectangle.
- [ ] Score into 2.5 cm (1-inch) strips.
- [ ] Drizzle evenly with 4 tbsp olive oil.
- [ ] Bake 12–15 minutes, then broil 2–5 minutes until browned. Internal temp:
      72–74°C.  
       Make the salad
- [ ] Toss red onion, cherry tomatoes, parsley, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp pepper, 2 tsp
      sumac, and juice of 1/2 lemon. Set aside.
