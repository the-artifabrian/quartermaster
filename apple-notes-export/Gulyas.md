Gulyas

Ingredients

- [ ] 4
      tbsp ++[olive oil](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/olive-oil-glossary)++
- [ ] 700g stewing steak, cut into chunks
- [ ] 30g ++[plain flour](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/flour-glossary)++
- [ ] 2 ++[large onions,](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/onion-glossary)++ thinly
      sliced
- [ ] 2 ++[garlic cloves,](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/garlic-glossary)++ finely
      chopped
- [ ] 2 ++[red peppers,](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/pepper-glossary)++ deseeded
      and thinly sliced
- [ ] 2 tbsp tomato purée
- [ ] 2
      tbsp ++[paprika](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/paprika-glossary)++
- [ ] 2 ++[large tomatoes,](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/tomato-glossary)++ diced
- [ ] 2 bay leaves
- [ ] 2 carrots
- [ ] 2 potatoes
- [ ] 75ml dry white wine
- [ ] 300ml beef stock, homemade or shop-bought
- [ ] 2
      tbsp ++[flat-leaf parsley leaves](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/parsley-glossary)++
- [ ] 150ml soured cream

Instructions

- [ ] Heat oven to 160C.
- [ ] Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a
      flameproof ++[casserole dish](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/review/test-best-casserole-dishes)++ or
      heavy-based saucepan. Sprinkle 700g stewing steak chunks with 30g plain
      flour and brown well in three batches, adding an extra 1 tbsp oil for each
      batch. Set the browned meat aside.
- [ ] Add in the remaining 1 tbsp oil to the casserole dish, followed by 1 large
      thinly sliced onion, 2 finely chopped garlic cloves, 1 green pepper and 1
      red pepper, both finely sliced. Fry until softened, around 5-10 mins.
- [ ] Return the beef to the pan with 2 tbsp tomato purée and 2 tbsp paprika.
      Cook, stirring, for 2 mins.
- [ ] Add in 2 large diced tomatoes, 75ml dry white wine and 300ml beef stock.
      Cover and bake in the oven for 1 hr 30 mins - 2 hrs.
- [ ] Add carrots and potatoes, cook for 20-25 mins.
- [ ] Sprinkle over 2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley leaves and season well with salt
      and freshly ground pepper. Stir in 150ml soured cream and serve.
