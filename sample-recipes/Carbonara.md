# Carbonara

## Ingredients

- [ ] 200g guanciale or pancetta
- [ ] 50g pecorino cheese
- [ ] 50g parmesan
- [ ] 2 whole eggs + 2 egg yolks
- [ ] 200g spaghetti
- [ ] 1 garlic clove, crushed
- [ ] sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

- [ ] Bring a large pot of water to a boil; salt it well.
- [ ] Prep: chop pancetta; grate pecorino + parmesan and combine.
- [ ] In a bowl, whisk **2 whole eggs + 2 yolks** with plenty of black pepper;
      stir in most of the cheese (save some to finish).
- [ ] Cook spaghetti until al dente; reserve a cup of pasta water.
- [ ] Meanwhile, sauté pancetta with a **bruised garlic clove** in a large pan
      over medium heat until crisp; remove/discard garlic.
- [ ] Add drained spaghetti to the pancetta pan (a little water clinging to it
      is good). Take the pan **off the heat**.
- [ ] Pour in egg/cheese mixture and toss vigorously; loosen with splashes of
      hot pasta water until glossy and saucy.
- [ ] Taste and adjust salt if needed. Serve immediately with remaining cheese +
      more pepper.
