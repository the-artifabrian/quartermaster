Apple Crumble Pie

Ingredients

- [ ] 80g sugar
- [ ] 1 lemon zest
- [ ] 280g wheat flour
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 100g butter
- [ ] 5g baking powder
- [ ] 8 - 10 apples
- [ ] 20g butter
- [ ] 1 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] ½ tsp ginger (optional)
- [ ] lemon juice (to taste)
- [ ] 50g sugar

   
 Instructions

- [ ] Mix sugar and lemon zest until it turns sandy.
- [ ] Scrape down the sides and add flour, egg and the cold butter and baking
      powder.
- [ ] Transfer about half of the mixture onto the base of a pan lined with
      parchment paper.
- [ ] Press down well with the back of a spoon, also forming the edges.
- [ ] Put the apples (cleaned and chopped), the sugar, the lemon juice, and the
      butter in a pan and cook down until a little liquid remains.
- [ ] Spread the apple mixture evenly over the base.
- [ ] Crumble the remaining pastry roughly with your hands over the apple
      mixture.
- [ ] Bake in a preheated static oven at 180°C for about 30 minutes.
- [ ] Remove from the oven, optionally dust with powdered sugar, and serve.
