Bolognese

Ingredients

- [ ] 500g ground beef
- [ ] 500g ground pork (shoulder)
- [ ] 1 large onion
- [ ] 2 carrots
- [ ] 2 stalks celery
- [ ] 3tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 4oz pancetta, chopped
- [ ] 50g tomato paste
- [ ] 200g white wine
- [ ] 500g chopped tomatoes
- [ ] 150g whole milk
- [ ] 400g chicken stock
- [ ] 4-5 garlic cloves
- [ ] 1tsp sugar
- [ ] 1tsp salt
- [ ] 20g balsamic vinegar

Instructions

- [ ] Chop veggies and garlic down a bit to large chunks
- [ ] Pulse in food processor until well chopped
- [ ] Preheat heavy bottomed pot (mine is 5.5 quarts)
- [ ] Add in 3 TBSP olive oil and saute vegetables with 2 pinches of salt
- [ ] Cook 4-5 minutes until liquid is released and vegetable are starting to
      take on color
- [ ] Meanwhile spread meat out on sheet tray and roast at 450 degrees until
      well browned and cooked through, this works under broiler well also, more
      caramelization there.
- [ ] After veggies are sweated for 4-5 mins. Add in tomato paste, bloom and fry
      for 1 minute, then deglaze with white wine, cook down by at least half
      then add in canned tomato.
- [ ] Bring to simmer and add milk.
- [ ] Crumble roasted meats by hand till very small and well broken down, add to
      tomato sauce
- [ ] Add in stock and bring to simmer, add 2 pinches salt
- [ ] Cook in 300 degree oven partially covered for 1 hour
- [ ] Remove lid and cook at 325 degrees additional 45 minutes, stirring once or
      twice.
- [ ] Remove from oven, skim fat off if there is a gratuitous amount and then
      add in balsamic, sugar, and salt
- [ ] Taste for seasoning adjusting to make as balanced and bright as possible.
      Serve with al dente tagliatelle or spaghetti, wine from central or
      northern Italy (for drinking), and a lot of parmesan
