Overnight pizza dough

Ingredients

- [ ] Water - 425g cold
- [ ] Salt - 15g
- [ ] Yeast - 0.5g
- [ ] Flour - 650g

Instructions

- [ ] Mix all ingredients in a bowl until a shaggy dough forms.
- [ ] Cover and rest for 10 minutes.
- [ ] Stretch and fold 1
- [ ] Stretch and fold 2
- [ ] Coil fold 1
- [ ] Coil fold 2
- [ ] Cover and let the dough proof at 20-24°C for 12-16 hours.
- [ ] Divide the dough into 4 x 275g pieces. Ball them up and place them into a
      proofing tray. Rest for 2 hours, then you can use it for baking.
