Tarkonyos Csirkeraguleves

Ingredients

- [ ] 600-700g chicken breast
- [ ] 3 tbsp sunflower oil
- [ ] 3 medium carrots, evenly diced
- [ ] 2 medium parsley roots, evenly diced
- [ ] 1 small kohlrabi, diced
- [ ] 1 small piece celeriac (about 1/4 of a bulb), diced
- [ ] 2 medium onions, finely chopped
- [ ] 300g green peas (fresh or frozen)
- [ ] 1.5 tsp dried tarragon (or 1 tbsp fresh, finely chopped), plus a pinch
      more at the end if needed
- [ ] 200g sour cream
- [ ] 1 tbsp flour
- [ ] 1tbsp white vinegar
- [ ] 2–3 bouillon cubes or equivalent stock powder
- [ ] 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- [ ] 1.5–1.7 L water (or light chicken stock)
- [ ] Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

- [ ] Dice the chicken and vegetables evenly, finely chop the onion, and chop
      the parsley.
- [ ] Soften the onion in hot oil with a pinch of salt, then add the chicken and
      sauté until just white.
- [ ] Add the diced vegetables and peas, then the bouillon cubes and
      water/stock, and simmer gently until everything is tender.
- [ ] Add tarragon, simmer briefly, then stir in the sour cream with flour over
      low heat without boiling hard.
- [ ] Season with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and a bit more tarragon if needed.
- [ ] Stir in the chopped parsley, let the soup rest a couple of minutes, and
      serve.
