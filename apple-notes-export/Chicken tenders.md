Chicken tenders

Ingredients

Marinade

- [ ] 4 chicken breasts or 1kg tenderloins
- [ ] 480g buttermilk
- [ ] 1 tbsp paprika
- [ ] 1 tbsp garlic powder
- [ ] 1 tbsp italian seasoning
- [ ] 1 tsp msg
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 2 tbsp hot sauce  
       Dredge
- [ ] 190g all-purpose flour
- [ ] 60g corn starch
- [ ] 1 tbsp paprika
- [ ] 1 tbsp garlic powder  
       For the Cane’s Sauce
- [ ] 170g mayonnaise
- [ ] 85g ketchup
- [ ] 1 ½ tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- [ ] 1 tsp ground black pepper
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 0.5 tsp salt

Instructions

- [ ] Slice chicken into tenders (if using breasts) and pat dry.
- [ ] Whisk buttermilk with paprika, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, MSG, and
      hot sauce.
- [ ] Add chicken to marinade, cover, and refrigerate 2–12 hours.
- [ ] Mix dredge: flour + cornstarch + paprika + garlic powder in a shallow
      bowl.
- [ ] Remove chicken from marinade, let excess drip off, then coat thoroughly in
      dredge.
- [ ] Rest coated chicken on a rack/plate 5–10 minutes to help the coating set.
- [ ] Fry in 175–180°C oil until golden and cooked through (about 3–5 min per
      side; ~74°C internal).
- [ ] Drain on a rack (not paper towels) and lightly salt if needed.
- [ ] Stir together sauce ingredients and chill 15–30 minutes before serving.
