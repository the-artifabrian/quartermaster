Classic American Deli Style Egg Salad

Ingredients

- [ ] 10 large eggs
- [ ] 100g (about 7 tablespoons) mayonnaise
- [ ] 20g (about 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon) Dijon mustard
- [ ] 6g (about 1 tablespoon + 2 teaspoons) minced chives
- [ ] 6g (about 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon) chopped dill
- [ ] 10g (about 2 teaspoons) white distilled vinegar
- [ ] 4g (about 3/4 teaspoon) salt
- [ ] 2g (about 3/4 teaspoon) black pepper
- [ ] 2g (about 3/4 teaspoon) paprika
- [ ] 6-8g (about 3/4 to 1 stalk) finely chopped celery

Instructions

- [ ] Press the 10 eggs through a wire rack to “dice” them evenly.
- [ ] In a separate bowl, combine mayo, Dijon, minced chives, chopped dill,
      vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, paprika, and chopped celery.
- [ ] Gently fold the mayo mixture into the diced hard boiled eggs until evenly
      coated.
- [ ] Crumble the last 2 reserved egg yolks into the mixture to absorb excess
      moisture and provide texture.
- [ ] To make each sandwich, spread Durkees famous sauce onto two slices of
      sourdough bread.
- [ ] Add washed green leaf lettuce on one slice of bread and spoon 3-4 large
      spoonfuls of egg salad on top.
