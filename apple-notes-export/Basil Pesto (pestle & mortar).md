Basil Pesto (pestle & mortar)

Ingredients

- [ ] 1 clove of garlic
- [ ] tiny pinch of coarse salt
- [ ] 15g pistacchio
- [ ] 40g basil
- [ ] 1.5 tbsp grated parmigiano reggiano
- [ ] 2-3 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

- [ ] Start with Garlic and Salt: Place the small garlic clove and a tiny pinch
      of coarse salt in the mortar. Pound and grind them with the pestle until
      you have a smooth paste.
- [ ] Add Nuts: Add the 1 tablespoon of pine nuts or pistachios to the mortar.
      Continue to pound and grind with the pestle, working them into the garlic
      paste.
- [ ] Incorporate Basil: Add the basil leaves a small handful at a time. Use a
      circular motion with the pestle, grinding and crushing the leaves against
      the sides of the mortar. Continue adding basil and grinding until it
      breaks down into a thick green paste.
- [ ] Add Cheese: Stir in the grated Parmigiano Reggiano and Pecorino cheese
      with the pestle or a spoon.
- [ ] Slowly Add Olive Oil: Gradually add the extra-virgin olive oil in a slow,
      steady stream (a "filo"). Work it in with the pestle to create a creamy
      emulsion. Add enough oil to reach your desired texture, starting with 2
      tablespoons and adding up to 3 if needed.
- [ ] Taste and Adjust: Taste the pesto and add more salt if needed.
