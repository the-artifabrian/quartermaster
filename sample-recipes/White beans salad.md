# White beans salad  
  
Ingredients  
- [ ] 2 cans bianchi di spagna  
- [ ] 150g tomatoes, diced  
- [ ] 1/2 medium red onion, finely diced  
- [ ] 1 kapia pepper, diced  
- [ ] 1 cucumber, diced  
- [ ] 2-3 celery stalks, diced  
- [ ] 1 bunch parsley, finely chopped  
- [ ] 75g feta, crumbled  
- [ ] 7-8 olives, diced  
- [ ] olive oil  
- [ ] lemon juice  
- [ ] big pinch of salt  
- [ ] (optional) avocado, grated garlic, mustard, capers etc.
