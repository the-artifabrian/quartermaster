Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

- [ ] 500g whole milk
- [ ] 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
- [ ] 1 tsp brown sugar
- [ ] 40g dark chocolate (70-75%)
- [ ] 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] Pinch of salt
- [ ] 1 tsp corn starch

Instructions

- [ ] In a small bowl, whisk cocoa, brown sugar, salt, and cornstarch with 2–3
      tbsp of the milk into a smooth slurry.
- [ ] Heat the remaining milk in a saucepan until steaming (don’t boil).
- [ ] Whisk in the slurry and cook, whisking, 1–2 minutes until slightly
      thickened and smooth.
- [ ] Remove from heat; add dark chocolate and vanilla, whisk until fully melted
      and glossy (blend briefly if you want it extra smooth).
- [ ] Taste and adjust sweetness/thickness with a little more sugar or a splash
      more milk.
