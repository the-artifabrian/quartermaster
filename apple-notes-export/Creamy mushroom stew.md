Creamy mushroom stew

Ingredients

- [ ] 400g brown mushrooms, like cremini or portobello
- [ ] 400g wild mushrooms, like chanterelle or porcini
- [ ] 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
- [ ] 2 tbsp butter
- [ ] 1 large onion, diced
- [ ] Salt and pepper
- [ ] 1/2 tsp chopped thyme
- [ ] 1tbsp flour
- [ ] 200ml milk
- [ ] 100g sour cream
- [ ] parsley for garnishing
- [ ] Polenta, for serving (optional)

Instructions

- [ ] Preparing the mushrooms:
  - [ ] Clean the porcini mushrooms well and cut them into medium-sized pieces.
        If using frozen mushrooms, thaw them first.
- [ ] Sautéing the mushrooms:
  - [ ] In a pan, melt the butter and add the oil. Add the finely chopped onion
        and sauté until it becomes translucent.
  - [ ] Add the porcini mushrooms and cook them on medium heat until the water
        they release evaporates and they turn slightly golden, about 10-15
        minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
- [ ] Making the sauce:
  - [ ] In a separate saucepan, add the flour and cook it on low heat for 1-2
        minutes, stirring constantly to avoid burning.
  - [ ] Gradually add the milk, stirring continuously with a whisk to prevent
        lumps.
  - [ ] Once the sauce starts to thicken, add the sour cream and mix well.
- [ ] Finishing the ciulama:
  - [ ] Add the sautéed mushrooms to the milk and flour sauce and stir well.
        Cook everything together for another 5-10 minutes, until the ciulama
        reaches your desired consistency.
- [ ] Serving:
  - [ ] Garnish with chopped dill or parsley, if you like, and serve the ciulama
        warm, alongside polenta or fresh bread.
