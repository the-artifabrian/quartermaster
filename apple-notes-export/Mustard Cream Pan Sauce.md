Mustard Cream Pan Sauce

Ingredients

- [ ] 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- [ ] 2 tsp whole grain mustard
- [ ] 90ml double cream (heavy cream)
- [ ] 1/2 small shallot or onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 40ml white wine
- [ ] 40ml chicken stock
- [ ] 10g unsalted butter
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste
- [ ] Fresh parsley, chopped (optional for garnish)

Instructions

- [ ] In the same pan, add the butter and let it melt.
- [ ] Add the chopped shallot and sauté until softened and translucent, about
      2-3 minutes.
- [ ] Pour in the white wine to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits
      from the bottom. Let it simmer and reduce by half, about 2 minutes.
- [ ] Add the chicken stock and let it simmer and reduce by half, about 3-4
      minutes.
- [ ] Stir in the Dijon mustard and mix well.
- [ ] Lower the heat to medium and pour in the double cream, stirring
      continuously.
- [ ] Let the sauce simmer gently until it thickens to your desired consistency,
      about 3-4 minutes.
- [ ] Season with salt and pepper to taste.
