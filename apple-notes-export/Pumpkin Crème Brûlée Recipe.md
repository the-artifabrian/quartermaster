Pumpkin Crème Brûlée Recipe

Ingredients

- [ ] 250g pumpkin puree
- [ ] 500ml heavy cream
- [ ] 100g sugar (plus more for the caramelized top)
- [ ] 5 egg yolks
- [ ] 1tsp vanilla extract
- [ ] 1/2tsp ground cinnamon
- [ ] 1/4tsp ground nutmeg
- [ ] Pinch of salt

Instructions

- [ ] Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 150°C.
- [ ] Mix Pumpkin Puree: In a bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, cinnamon,
      nutmeg, and salt.
- [ ] Heat Cream: In a saucepan, gently heat the heavy cream until it's warm but
      not boiling.
- [ ] Combine Egg Yolks and Sugar: In another bowl, whisk together the egg yolks
      and sugar until smooth.
- [ ] Combine Mixtures: Gradually whisk the warm cream into the egg mixture.
      Then, stir in the vanilla extract and the pumpkin mixture until well
      combined.
- [ ] Prepare Ramekins: Pour the mixture into ramekins. Place the ramekins in a
      large baking dish and fill the dish with boiling water halfway up the
      sides of the ramekins.
- [ ] Bake: Bake for about 30-40 minutes or until the edges are set but the
      center is still slightly wobbly.
- [ ] Chill: Remove from the oven, let cool to room temperature, then
      refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
- [ ] Caramelize Sugar: Before serving, sprinkle sugar over each crème brûlée
      and use a kitchen torch to caramelize the top. If you don't have a torch,
      you can place them under the broiler for a minute or two.
