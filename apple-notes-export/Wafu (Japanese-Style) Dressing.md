Wafu (Japanese-Style) Dressing

Ingredients

- [ ] 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- [ ] 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- [ ] 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (e.g., canola or grapeseed)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sugar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon grated ginger
- [ ] 1 teaspoon grated garlic (optional)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

1. Combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, vegetable oil, sugar, ginger,
   and garlic in a bowl.
2. Whisk until the sugar dissolves and the dressing is emulsified.
3. Stir in toasted sesame seeds for extra nuttiness.
4. Pour over your salad greens and toss lightly.
