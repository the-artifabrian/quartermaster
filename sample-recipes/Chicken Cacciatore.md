# Chicken Cacciatore  
  
## Ingredients  
- [ ] 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs  
- [ ] Salt and black pepper  
- [ ] 2 tbsp olive oil  
- [ ] 1 large onion, sliced  
- [ ] 2 bell peppers (any color), cut into strips  
- [ ] 4 garlic cloves, minced  
- [ ] 1 can crushed tomatoes  
- [ ] 120ml dry white wine  
- [ ] 250g mushrooms  
- [ ] 1 tsp italian seasoning  
- [ ] 2 bay leaves  
- [ ] (optional) 1 tbsp capers or pitted olives  
- [ ] Fresh basil or parsley for garnish  
  
  
  
## Instructions  
- [ ] **Brown chicken**: Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high. Brown chicken 4-5 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.  
- [ ] Cook mushrooms: Reduce heat to medium. Cook mushrooms until lightly browned.  
- [ ] **Cook vegetables**: Add onion and peppers, cook 5 minutes until softened. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.  
- [ ] **Build sauce**: Add tomatoes, broth, oregano, basil, bay leaves, and red pepper flakes. Stir and bring to simmer.  
- [ ] **Add chicken**: Return chicken to pan skin-side up. Spoon sauce over. Cover, reduce heat to medium-low, simmer 25 minutes.  
- [ ] **Finish**: Remove bay leaf. Garnish with fresh herbs. Serve over pasta, rice, or with crusty bread.
