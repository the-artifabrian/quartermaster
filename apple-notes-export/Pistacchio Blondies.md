Pistacchio Blondies

Ingredients

- [ ] 1 large egg, at room temperature
- [ ] 1 large egg yolk
- [ ] 100g light brown sugar
- [ ] 60g granulated sugar
- [ ] 90g butter browned
- [ ] 1 tsp ++[pure vanilla extract](https://www.amazon.com/Simply-Organic-Vanilla-Extract-Packaging/dp/B0BK1TRFXB?crid=1NCWYBBK5YLPX&keywords=bourbon+vanilla+extract&qid=1679083122&sprefix=bourbon+vanilla+extract%2Caps%2C107&sr=8-12&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=7054b12999b042a1653150e28fe99df8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++
- [ ] 120g all-purpose flour
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon salt
- [ ] ⅛ teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 1tsp matcha powder (optional)
- [ ] 1/4 tsp almond extract (optional)
- [ ] 80g coarsely
      chopped ++[dark chocolate](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B008RBC1JM/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B008RBC1JM&linkCode=as2&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=aaef8d3e5f5d1bbaa12c64259e09205c%22%3EBelgian%20Dark%20Couverture%20Chocolate%20Semisweet%20Callets,%2054.5%25%20-%205.5%20Lbs%3C/a%3E%3Cimg%20src=%22//ir-na.amazon-adsystem.com/e/ir?t=cmarsi08-20&l=am2&o=1&a=B008RBC1JM%22%20width=%221%22%20height=%221%22%20border=%220%22%20alt=%22%22%20style=%22border:none%20!important;%20margin:0px%20!important;)++ 
- [ ] 60g ++[roasted salted pistachios (no shell)](https://www.amazon.com/Wonderful-Pistachios-No-Shell-Roasted-Salted/dp/B004HZFASG?crid=2I59ZFXTWSKU4&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.Vb1BJieWAU-hxg0Zl-uaYcRyBwV2iQA2i_FLrrP-AfmikR5JVDEJjhriVSXJUa7-GFIUUM_Zw43CN6bO1e78ZiL7QVHQLRT6VOW4sBfkxDx1fFENPVsesTrtoNhiRm927YMy4wF7ZPrBD3d7Czvc6seyULksgucWpM1pjbQVnKB8Pia5s1On5pNE6-y2AA6GM7bAE34Ihg0cotq_YGyOWNQXzQW-x5rjX2ouayxfZEFNVDGe71b_BLQZKpV2c4Pr2x_fG-5pSpwliUuytt8HwAQJeY8qhAdjMG-Kd9YhfeM.b4jNBdp8Q0j0ZNnh3jyBWPAocSYf_RR-EjLPvatiSQY&dib_tag=se&keywords=roasted+pistachios&qid=1713551624&sprefix=roasted+pistachios%2Caps%2C109&sr=8-2&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=d7763d2ab56c6e95aa700bf330243425&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++ for
      topping

Instructions

- [ ] Preheat the oven to 160C. Line
      a++[ 8x8-inch metal baking pan](https://www.amazon.com/USA-Pan-1120BW-AP-1-Allergy-Nonstick/dp/B07DXR8S51?crid=28DRAQ3V452PK&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9.ltfjsCFutu3Ls6nkY2qUOKg4wEwF_x-xcfRlO5JA6zRwblwul2bUcrWCDf8j5XrFKv8VQizGvaD-R06DEf1zFzTR6WFMZoNBk5opHNNMh_jYc6tPeLsaK3RCJqdDg0h4nAuZV-IX0OTdv8w69jNIBkT1BcWoBFYOBkw091MlzgT_hA70R9UVqboKpmcV8O4BLZehIc1pKxFMigxa12Bdu_N54NSMb8X3u-aCVgfunZCzhof99QWwNNkML-oHIR__0B1g2Mwcr8XlX7SzoHGQzY3Yt35c7sKNOxMFresxBsg._QIXl3mqZGbWSfQRSgoHQFueb2bA1znAU3H3u67zdVw&dib_tag=se&keywords=8%2Binch%2Bsquare%2Bbaking%2Bpan&qid=1713549347&sprefix=8%2Binch%2Bsquare%2Bbaking%2Bpan%2Caps%2C98&sr=8-77&th=1&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=5369b78de37bb47a7e5d6489bafd707f&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++ with ++[parchment paper](https://amzn.to/3C6DeC8)++,
      leaving a 2-inch overhang at each side for easy removal. 
- [ ] Prepare the blondie batter. Place egg, yolk, both sugars, vanilla extract
      and almond extract in a large bowl and mix well until smooth and
      creamy-looking. Add melted butter and mix in it. Add pistachio paste and
      mix it through. 
- [ ] Combine flour, matcha powder, salt and baking powder in a medium bowl and
      whisk well to blend evenly. Add this dry mixture to the wet ingredients
      and fold them in until mostly incorporated but before all of the flour is
      absorbed (i.e. a few streaks of flour are still remaining). Add the
      chocolate chunks and fold them through until evenly combined. 
- [ ] Use a ++[small offset spatula](https://amzn.to/3R3AyKy)++ to spread the
      thick batter into the prepared pan, sprinkle pistachios on top then bake
      for 22-25 minutes until golden around the edges and still slightly soft in
      the middle. Transfer pan to a wire rack and let cool completely. Slice and
      enjoy!
