Goma (Sesame) Dressing

Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds (or tahini for convenience)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons Japanese mayonnaise (e.g., Kewpie)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- [ ] 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sugar (or honey)
- [ ] 1 tablespoon water (to thin, if needed)

Instructions

1. Toast the sesame seeds in a dry pan over medium heat until fragrant (if using
   whole seeds).
2. Grind the toasted sesame seeds in a mortar and pestle or food processor until
   they form a paste.
3. Mix the sesame paste (or tahini) with the mayonnaise, soy sauce, rice
   vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, and water.
4. Taste and adjust sweetness or saltiness as needed.
5. Drizzle over fresh greens like lettuce, cucumber, and shredded carrots.
