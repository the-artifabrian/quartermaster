Poolish Pizza

Ingredients

Poolish

- [ ] Water - 250g
- [ ] Yeast - 0.4g
- [ ] Flour - 250g

Final Dough

- [ ] Water - 175g (very cold)
- [ ] Salt - 14g
- [ ] Yeast - 0.25g
- [ ] Flour - 400g

Instructions

For the Poolish

- [ ] Mix the water, flour and yeast together to form a smooth paste. Cover the
      container and let ferment at 21°C for 7-8 hours. The Poolish is ready when
      it has tripled in size, and it’s bubbly, but hasn't collapsed at all.

For the Final Dough

- [ ] Add the second measurement of water to a large mixing bowl.
- [ ] Add the Poolish, second measurement of yeast and salt, then stir the
      mixture with a fork to combine evenly.
- [ ] Add the second measurement of flour and mix with the fork until you have a
      shaggy pizza dough. Next, use a wet hand and continue mixing the dough
      until it is relatively homogenous. Cover and let the dough rest for 10-15
      minutes.
- [ ] Stretch and fold
- [ ] Coil fold
- [ ] Form the dough into a ball, place it into a lightly oiled container, cover
      it, and let the dough proof at 21°C for 2 hours.
- [ ] Next, divide the dough into 4 x 275 gram pieces. Ball them up and place
      them into lightly oiled container. Rest for 30 minutes, then place the
      dough in the refrigerator overnight to cold ferment. The dough is ready to
      bake for dinner the following evening. Remember to pull the dough 1-1.5
      hours before stretching.
