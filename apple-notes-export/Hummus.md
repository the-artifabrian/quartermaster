Hummus

Ingredients

- [ ] 500g canned chickpeas, drained
- [ ] Juice of 1 lemon
- [ ] 6-7 medium cloves of garlic
- [ ] 120g tahini
- [ ] 60ml olive oil
- [ ] half a tsp salt, more to taste
- [ ] freshly ground pepper
- [ ] 3-5 tbsp ice-cold water (as needed for consistency)

Instructions

- [ ] Drain the chickpeas and rinse under cold water.
- [ ] In a food processor, blend the lemon juice and garlic cloves until the
      garlic is finely minced. Strain out the garlic and leep the lemon-garlic
      liquid.
- [ ] Add the tahini to the lemon-garlic mixture and blend until it forms a
      thick paste. Gradually add ice-cold water to achieve a smooth, creamy
      consistency.
- [ ] Add the chickpeas, olive oil, salt to the food processor. Blend for about
      2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
- [ ] Taste and adjust seasoning as needed, adding more salt or lemon juice if
      desired.
- [ ] If the hummus is too thick, add more ice-cold water, one tablespoon at a
      time, until you reach your desired consistency.
- [ ] Serve the hummus in a bowl, drizzled with olive oil and any additional
      toppings you prefer.
