# Gemuse Kebab

## Ingredients

- [ ] 800g boneless chicken thighs

### Chicken Marinade

- [x] 1 tsp sweet paprika
- [x] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [x] 1 tsp cumin
- [x] 1 tsp coriander
- [x] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [x] 1 tsp oregano
- [x] 1/2 tsp black pepper
- [x] 1 tsp salt
- [x] 1 tbsp olive oil
- [x] 1 tbsp lemon juice

### Yogurt Sauce

- [x] 200g greek yogurt
- [x] 40g mayo
- [x] 2 cloves of garlic, grated
- [x] squeeze of lemon
- [x] pinch of salt
- [x] parsley
- [x] dill
- [x] water to loosen mixture

### Vegetables

- [x] 2 medium red onions, cut into chunks
- [x] 2 large bell peppers
- [x] 1 large zucchini (or 2 small)

### Salad

- [ ] white cabbage
- [ ] carrots

## Instructions

- [ ] Mix all marinade spices + salt with olive oil + lemon, coat the chicken
      thighs, and marinate 30 min–overnight.
- [ ] Make yogurt sauce: stir yogurt + mayo + grated garlic + lemon + salt +
      chopped parsley + dill, then add a splash of water to desired drizzle
      thickness.
- [ ] Heat oven to 220°C (or 200°C fan) and preheat a tray/sheet pan while you
      prep.
- [ ] Chop onions into chunks, slice peppers into thick strips, and cut zucchini
      into chunky half-moons.
- [ ] Toss veg with a pinch of salt (and a tiny drizzle of oil if you want),
      then spread on the hot tray.
- [ ] Nestle the marinated chicken among the veg in a single layer (don’t
      crowd).
- [ ] Roast 20–25 min, flipping once, until chicken is cooked through and edges
      are charred.
- [ ] Optional finish: blast 2–3 min under the grill/broiler for extra
      doner-style browning.
- [ ] Rest chicken 5 min, then slice thinly and toss back through the roasted
      veg juices.
- [ ] Shred white cabbage + carrots for a quick salad (salt + lemon squeeze).
- [ ] Serve chicken + veg over the salad and drizzle heavily with yogurt sauce.
