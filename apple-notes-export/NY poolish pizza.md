NY poolish pizza

Ingredients  
Poolish (100% hydration)

- [ ] Flour: 250g
- [ ] Water: 250g
- [ ] Yeast (IDY): 0.5 g  
       Final dough
- [ ] Flour: 400g
- [ ] Water: 160g (very cold)
- [ ] Salt: 12.5g
- [ ] Yeast (IDY): 0.25g  

Instructions

- [ ] Mix poolish until combined, cover, and ferment at room temp until domed
      and bubbly (about 10–14 hours).
- [ ] For final dough, mix poolish with water, flour, salt, yeast and mix until
      smooth and moderately developed.
- [ ] Cover and rest the dough 20–30 minutes at room temp.
- [ ] Divide into 4 balls, place in lightly oiled covered containers, and
      refrigerate 48 hours.
- [ ] Remove dough balls 2–3 hours before baking and let them warm/relax until
      puffy.
- [ ] Bake in the Arc XL on a moderately hot deck (~315–370°C) with managed
      flame for a slightly longer bake to fully brown and crisp the bottom.
