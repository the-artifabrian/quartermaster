Spaetzle

Ingredients

- [ ] 240g all-purpose flour
- [ ] 1.5tsp salt
- [ ] ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
- [ ] 180g whole milk
- [ ] 3 large eggs
- [ ] 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Instructions

- [ ] Whisk flour, salt and nutmeg together in large bowl. Whisk milk and eggs
      together in second bowl. Slowly whisk milk mixture into flour mixture
      until smooth. Cover and let rest for 15 to 30 minutes.
- [ ] While batter rests, use scissors to poke about forty ¼-inch holes in
      bottom of disposable pan. Bring 4 quarts water to boil in Dutch oven.
- [ ] Add 1 tablespoon salt to boiling water and set prepared disposable pan on
      top of Dutch oven. Transfer half of batter to disposable pan. Use spatula
      to scrape batter across holes, letting batter fall into water. Boil until
      spaetzle float, about 1 minute. Using spider skimmer or slotted spoon,
      transfer spaetzle to colander set in large bowl to drain. Repeat with
      remaining batter.
- [ ] Discard any accumulated water in bowl beneath colander. Pour spaetzle into
      now-empty bowl. Add melted butter and toss to combine. Serve.
