Baba Ganoush

Ingredients

- [ ] 900g Italian eggplants (about 2 small-to-medium)
- [ ] 2 medium garlic cloves, pressed or minced
- [ ] 30 ml lemon juice, more to taste
- [ ] 60 g tahini
- [ ] 80 ml extra-virgin olive oil , plus more for brushing/garnish
- [ ] 2 tbsp chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, plus extra for garnish (about 8 g)
- [ ] ¾ tsp fine salt, to taste
- [ ] ¼ tsp ground cumin
- [ ] Pinch of smoked paprika, for garnish
