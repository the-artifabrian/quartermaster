# Beef and Broccoli Stirfry  
  
## Ingredients  
Meat and Marinade  
- [ ] 600g steak  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon soy sauce  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon peanut oil (or vegetable oil)  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon cornstarch  
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sugar  
- [ ] 0.5 teaspoon baking soda   
Sauce  
- [ ] 140g water (or chicken stock)  
- [ ] 2 tablespoons Shaoxing wine   
- [ ] 2 tablespoons soy sauce  
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce  
- [ ] 2 teaspoons sugar  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon rice vinegar  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon cornstarch  
- [ ] 4-5 garlic cloves, grated  
- [ ] 2 teaspoons ginger, grated  
- [ ] 1tsp sesame oil  
- [ ] 0.5 tsp msg  
Stir-fry  
- [ ] 600g broccoli, cut to bite-size florets  
- [ ] 1 medium onion, cut into strips  
- [ ] 1 bell peppers, cut into strips  
- [ ] 1 tablespoon peanut oil (or vegetable oil)   
  
  
## Instructions  
- [ ] Slice beef against the grain into 0.5 cm slices or 1 cm sticks; toss with 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp peanut oil, 1 tbsp cornstarch, and 0.5 tsp baking soda; marinate 10 minutes.  
- [ ] In a bowl, whisk together 120 ml water, 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp dark soy, 2 tsp sugar, 1 tbsp cornstarch, 3 grated garlic cloves, and 2 tsp grated ginger.  
- [ ] Add 60 ml water to a large skillet over medium‑high, bring to a boil, add broccoli, cover, and steam until just tender and water evaporates, about 1 minute; transfer broccoli to a plate and wipe pan dry.  
- [ ] Add 1 tbsp peanut oil to the hot pan, spread beef in a single layer, sear 30 seconds without stirring, then flip and stir‑fry until lightly charred but still slightly pink inside.  
- [ ] Add onion and bell pepper strips and stir‑fry until just starting to soften and pick up color.  
- [ ] Return broccoli to the pan, stir the sauce to re‑dissolve cornstarch, pour it in, and cook, stirring, until thickened and glossy, about 1 minute; serve immediately.
