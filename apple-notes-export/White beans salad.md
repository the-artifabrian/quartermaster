White beans salad

Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cans bianchi di spagna
- [ ] 150g tomatoes, diced
- [ ] 1/2 medium red onion, finely diced
- [ ] 1 kapia pepper, diced
- [ ] 1 cucumber, diced
- [ ] 2-3 celery stalks, diced
- [ ] 1 bunch parsley, finely chopped
- [ ] 75g feta, crumbled
- [ ] 7-8 olives, diced
- [ ] olive oil
- [ ] lemon juice
- [ ] big pinch of salt
- [ ] (optional) avocado, grated garlic, mustard, capers etc.

Instructions

- [ ] Drain and rinse the white beans well, then let them dry off a bit.
- [ ] Dice tomatoes, red onion, kapia pepper, cucumber, and celery; finely chop
      parsley.
- [ ] In a large bowl, combine beans + all chopped veg + parsley.
- [ ] Add diced olives and crumbled feta.
- [ ] Dress with olive oil + lemon juice + a big pinch of salt (start light,
      adjust).
- [ ] Toss gently so the beans and feta don’t break up too much.
- [ ] Taste and tweak: more lemon/salt/olive oil as needed.
- [ ] Optional add-ins: grated garlic, a little mustard, capers, or avocado;
      toss again.
- [ ] Rest 10–15 minutes (or chill) to let flavors meld, then serve.
