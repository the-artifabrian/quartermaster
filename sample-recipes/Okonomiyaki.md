# Okonomiyaki

## Ingredients

- [ ] 100 ml water
- [ ] 10g dashi powder
- [ ] 80 g plain flour
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 240g finely chopped cabbage
- [ ] 1 chopped green onion
- [ ] 80 g thinly sliced pork belly or bacon
- [ ] Aonori to garnish
- [ ] okonomiyaki or tonkatsu sauce
- [ ] kewpie mayo
