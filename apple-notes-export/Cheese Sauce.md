Cheese Sauce

Ingredients

- [ ] 500ml milk
- [ ] 50g flour
- [ ] 50g butter
- [ ] 100g grated cheddar
- [ ] 50g grated havarti

Instructions

- [ ] Pour 500ml milk into a large saucepan and add 4 tbsp plain flour and 50g
      butter.
- [ ] Turn on the heat to medium and start
      to ++[whisk](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/content/top-five-whisks)++ the
      mixture. Keep whisking fast as the butter melts and the mixture comes to
      the boil – the flour will disappear and the sauce will begin to thicken.
- [ ] Whisk for another 2 mins while the sauce bubbles then stir in 100g grated
      strong cheddar cheese (or 50g grated cheddar, 50g crumbled blue cheese)
      until melted.
