Brown Butter Hazelnut Pear Upside Down Cake

Ingredients

Hazelnut cake batter:

- [x] 84g unsalted butter
- [x] 110g light brown sugar
- [x] 65g granulated sugar
- [x] 2 large eggs at room temperature
- [x] 120g greek yogurt
- [x] 1tsp
      ++[pure vanilla extract](https://www.amazon.com/Simply-Organic-Vanilla-Extract-Packaging/dp/B0BK1TRFXB?crid=1NCWYBBK5YLPX&keywords=bourbon+vanilla+extract&qid=1679083122&sprefix=bourbon+vanilla+extract%2Caps%2C107&sr=8-12&linkCode=ll1&tag=cmarsi08-20&linkId=7054b12999b042a1653150e28fe99df8&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)++
- [x] 75g finely ground roasted hazelnuts (measured after grinding)
- [x] 142g all-purpose flour
- [x] 1 tsp baking powder
- [x] ¼ tsp baking soda
- [x] ¼ tsp salt
- [x] 120ml whole milk

For the Pears:

- [ ] 2 large ripe but firm pears

Instructions

Cake

- [x] Brown the butter, then set it aside to cool for 15 minutes.
- [x] Combine cooled brown butter, both types of sugar, eggs, sour cream, milk
      and vanilla in a large mixing bowl and whisk until smooth and creamy.
- [x] Mix in the ground hazelnuts.
- [x] Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a medium bowl and
      whisk to blend evenly.
- [x] Add it to the wet mixture and fold it in by hand with a spatula until
      mostly incorporated but a few streaks of flour remain.
- [x] Gently scrape the batter into the pan over the pears and spread it out
      evenly. Bake for 30-40 minutes until the cake is evenly browned and it
      feels firm to the touch. A toothpick inserted into the center should come
      out without any wet batter. Transfer pan to a wire rack and let cool
      completely. Once cool, invert the cake onto a serving plate and peel of
      the parchment.
