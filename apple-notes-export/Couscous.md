Couscous

Ingredients

- [ ] 2 tbsp olive oil
- [ ] 150g couscous
- [ ] 500g water
- [ ] Salt
- [ ] 2 large bunches of parsley, minced
- [ ] 1 tsp dried mint
- [ ] 1 large garlic clove, very finely chopped
- [ ] 1 red onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 8 cherry tomatoes, seeded and diced
- [ ] 1 english cucumber, diced
- [ ] lemon juice to taste
- [ ] black pepper

Instructions

- [ ] Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the
      couscous and stir until it turns a deep golden brown and smells toasted.
- [ ] Add the water and a pinch of salt and bring to a boil. Lower the heat to a
      simmer and cook, stirring often, until all the water has been absorbed, 10
      to 14 minutes. Let cool.
- [ ] Stir the remaining olive oil into the couscous. Add the parsley and mint,
      garlic, onion, tomatoes and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and
      pepper.
