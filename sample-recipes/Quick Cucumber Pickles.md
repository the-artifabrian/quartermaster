# Quick Cucumber Pickles

## Ingredients

- [x] 2 large fabio cucumbers
- [x] 322g water
- [x] 107g white vinegar (7-9 degrees)
- [x] 10g salt
- [x] 20-30g sugar
- [ ] 2g mustard seeds or coriander seeds
- [ ] 1-2 bay leaves
- [ ] 1g turmeric

## Instructions

- [ ] Thinly slice 2 large cucumbers (or cut into spears) and pack into a clean
      jar/container.
- [ ] Add optional spices to the jar: 2 g mustard or coriander seeds, 1–2 bay
      leaves, 1 g turmeric.
- [ ] In a bowl/jug, whisk 322 g water + 107 g white vinegar + 10 g salt + 20–30
      g sugar until fully dissolved.
- [ ] Pour the brine over cucumbers to fully submerge (top up with a splash of
      water if needed).
- [ ] Cover and chill 1–2 hours for “quick” pickles (best after ~12–24 hours).
- [ ] Keep refrigerated; they’ll stay good and crunchy about 5–7 days.
