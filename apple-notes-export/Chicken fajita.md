Chicken fajita

Ingredients:

- [ ] 800g-1kg chicken breast
- [ ] 1 medium onion
- [ ] 1 lime
- [ ] 3 bell peppers red, yellow, green or orange
- [ ] 3 tablespoons olive oil divided
- [ ] 1 tsp salt
- [ ] 1 tsp mediterranean herbs
- [ ] 1 tsp smoked paprika
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] ½ tsp onion powder
- [ ] ½ tsp black pepper
- [ ] ½ tsp cumin
- [ ] drizzle of oil

Instructions:

- [ ] Combine all ingredients in a bowl, grill or pan sear on high heat, serve.
