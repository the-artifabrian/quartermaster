Brown Butter Hazelnut Pear Upside Down Cake

Ingredients

- [ ] 84g unsalted butter
- [ ] 110g light brown sugar
- [ ] 65g granulated sugar
- [ ] 2 large eggs at room temperature
- [ ] 120g greek yogurt
- [ ] 1tsp pure vanilla extract
- [ ] 75g finely ground roasted hazelnuts (measured after grinding)
- [ ] 142g all-purpose flour
- [ ] 1 tsp baking powder
- [ ] ¼ tsp baking soda
- [ ] ¼ tsp salt
- [ ] 120ml whole milk
- [ ] 2 large ripe but firm pears

Instructions

- [ ] Brown the butter, then set it aside to cool for 15 minutes.
- [ ] Combine cooled brown butter, both types of sugar, eggs, sour cream, milk
      and vanilla in a large mixing bowl and whisk until smooth and creamy.
- [ ] Mix in the ground hazelnuts.
- [ ] Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a medium bowl and
      whisk to blend evenly.
- [ ] Add it to the wet mixture and fold it in by hand with a spatula until
      mostly incorporated but a few streaks of flour remain.
- [ ] Gently scrape the batter into the pan over the pears and spread it out
      evenly. Bake for 30-40 minutes until the cake is evenly browned and it
      feels firm to the touch. A toothpick inserted into the center should come
      out without any wet batter. Transfer pan to a wire rack and let cool
      completely. Once cool, invert the cake onto a serving plate and peel of
      the parchment.
