Italian Sausage Pizza

 Ingredients

Poolish Pre-Ferment

- [ ] 1g instant yeast (all yeast for the entire recipe)
- [ ] 150g bread flour
- [ ] 150g water

Pizza Dough (remaining after poolish)

- [ ] 410g high-protein bread flour
- [ ] 185g water
- [ ] 17g salt (3%)
- [ ] 11g sugar (2%)
- [ ] 11g extra virgin olive oil (2%)
- [ ] All of the poolish

Pizza Sauce

- [ ] 400g peeled canned tomatoes (ideally San Marzano)
- [ ] 4g salt
- [ ] 1g garlic powder
- [ ] 1g oregano
- [ ] 8g extra virgin olive oil

Hot Italian Sausage

- [ ] Pork mince — 100%
- [ ] Salt — 1.75%
- [ ] Black pepper — 0.25%
- [ ] Oregano — 0.12%
- [ ] Basil — 0.12%
- [ ] Chili flakes — 0.5%
- [ ] Hand-crushed fennel seeds — 0.9%
- [ ] Fresh garlic, minced — 1%
- [ ] Sugar — 0.5%
- [ ] Smoked paprika — 0.25%
- [ ] Red wine vinegar — 0.5%
- [ ] Ice-cold water — 3%

Toppings & Assembly

- [ ] Low-moisture mozzarella slices
- [ ] Pepperoni
- [ ] Pecorino cheese (grated)
- [ ] Dried oregano
- [ ] Chili flakes
- [ ] Extra virgin olive oil (for crust)
- [ ] Semolina flour (for dusting)
- [ ] Hot honey (optional)

Instructions

Day 1 — Poolish & Italian Sausage

Poolish: Mix all yeast with 150g flour and 150g water until wet and sticky.
Cover and leave at room temp for ~3 hours, then refrigerate overnight.  
Italian Sausage: Combine pork mince with all seasonings. Use crushed (not
ground) fennel seeds and fresh minced garlic — no substitutes. Mix very well,
bag it, and fridge overnight. 2–3 days is ideal. Freeze leftovers.

Day 2 — Dough

Mix: Add poolish + remaining 185g water to stand mixer, combine on low. Add 410g
flour, salt, sugar, and olive oil. Mix on speed 2 for 8 minutes. Rest 30 min
covered.  
Stretch & fold (×3): Pull each of the four sides up and fold over. Cover and
rest 30 min. Repeat twice more — dough should be very smooth.  
Shape & fridge: Divide into 2 balls, place in lightly oiled containers,
refrigerate overnight (up to 3 days).

Day 3 — Sauce, Assembly & Bake

Dough: Remove from fridge ~4 hours before baking to come to room temp.  
Sauce: Blitz all sauce ingredients with a stick blender using short pulses
(don't over-blend — broken seeds = bitter). Transfer to a squeeze bottle.

Assemble (cheese on bottom, sauce on top):

1. Stretch dough on semolina-dusted surface, place on pizza screen.
2. Lay mozzarella slices directly on dough.
3. Squeeze sauce over cheese — less than you think; it's enough.
4. Top with oregano, pecorino, pepperoni, and pinched-off pieces of Italian
   sausage.
5. Brush crust with olive oil.

Bake (high-heat oven): Flame on low. Pizza on screen to the back of oven.
Quarter-turn after ~1 min, again shortly after. Once crust sets, slide pizza off
screen onto oven floor, rotate until evenly browned.  
Finish: More pecorino, chili flakes, and optionally hot honey.
